Salvia Care Guide

Soil for Salvia

Well-Draining Soil

Use loamy soil that drains well to prevent waterlogging.

Watering

Regular Watering

Water moderately, allowing the soil to dry slightly between waterings.

Pot Size

Appropriate Size

Choose a pot that accommodates root growth and provides adequate drainage.

Colors

Salvia comes in various colors like purple, green, and red.

Benefits

Attracts Pollinators

Salvia flowers attract bees, butterflies, and hummingbirds to the garden.

Drought Tolerant

Salvia is relatively drought tolerant once established.

Non-Benefits

Overwatering can lead to root rot and damage for Salvia plants.
